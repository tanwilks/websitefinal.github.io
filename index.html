My Journey / Resume

  • 2012-2018

    A Childhood of Creation

    Since I could walk I would make videos. It became a real passion of mine when my friends and I began making trampoline edits. Every school video project went above and beyond criteria purely because we just loved to create! For 6 years I did all of my video projects in adobe premiere pro. I became fluent in the software long before I considered myself a professional.

  • Senior year 2019-2020

    Putting in the Hours

    My senior year I was injured and could no longer participate in competitive rock climbing, so I threw myself into videography! I create videos of almost every sport at the school. I went on to win the Live Event Recap Award at the Utah High School Broadcast awards for my work with sports highlight videos. After graduating with a 4.0 and honors I began getting my first video jobs. I did work for Frictitious and Sweat.

      Awards and Skills
    • Sports Videography - Awarded Best Event Recap at Utah High School Broadcast Awards
    • Photoshop
    • 4.0 GPA at Timpanogos High School

  • August 2020 - August 2022

    Missoinary Service

    For 2 years I served as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, in the Tacoma Washington Mission and in the country of Suriname. I made over 40 videos in my 2 years, some reaching over 80,000 views. It was a time of refinement in my skills, character and faith.
